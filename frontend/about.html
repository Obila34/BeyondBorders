Our Story

Beyond the Map.

At Beyond Borders, we believe the journey is as vital as the destination. We specialize in overland road trips that turn travelers into a community. From the rugged terrains of Northern Kenya to the coastal breezes of Zanzibar, we handle the logistics so you can focus on the experience.

Our Mission

The road matters as much as the destination.

Beyond Borders is Kenyan at heart and community-led by design. We build high-end group adventures that feel open, social, and carefully considered from departure to return.

Whether the route heads north through rugged terrain or east toward the coast and beyond, we keep the logistics disciplined so the spirit of the journey stays free.

What We Stand For
